Cat's Are From Space

Cat's are from space. How do I know? I'll show you with some examples from my two pet cats.

Ralph

Ralph is from Mars because she is always crying at the stars. Her eyes are like black holes: big, empty, and lacking soul.

Ralph's favorite thing about space?

She can share her favorite string with new alien friends.

Gigi

Gigi is from Venus because she is the meanest. Her fur is striped gray like the mixing colors of the Milky Way.

Gigi's favorite thing about space?

No one else is around to bother her.
