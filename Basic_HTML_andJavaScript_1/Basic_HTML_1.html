A pixie will now visit you in the middle of the night and steal your most prized possession!!!

Hide all your precious belongings in a safe if you want to keep them.

If you do not have a safe, you can purchase a lock box and place several heavy items on top of it and around it.

If you are unable to purchase a lock box, your only option of keeping any value from your prized possession is to sell it before the pixie arrives.

BEWARE:Do not stay up and try to fight/stop the pixie. They will seriously hurt you.
